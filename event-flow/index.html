FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book EventFlow?

We recommend 3–6 months for weddings and large events, 4–6 weeks for corporate or birthday events. We accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability — just reach out!

Do you manage destination events across India?

Absolutely! We specialize in Jaipur, Udaipur, Goa, Kerala, and select international destinations. Our Elite package includes comprehensive travel and accommodation coordination.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

60+ days: full refund. 30–60 days: 50% refund. Under 30 days: non-refundable but we offer flexible rescheduling. We always work to find the fairest solution for our clients.

Can I fully customize my event package?

Every single package is 100% customizable. We start with a detailed discovery session to understand your vision, then build a completely bespoke event plan around your preferences and budget.

Do you provide catering, photography & entertainment?

We partner with 50+ premium caterers and 30+ top photography/videography studios across India. Live entertainment, DJs, and celebrity artists are all coordinated through our single-point team.
